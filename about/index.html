Description

Amy and Samy Bouzaglo were recently featured in the season finale of 'Kitchen Nightmares'. Their appearance on the show followed by their adorable rant on Facebook ensured that they caught the attention of the Internet. These believers of the ALMIGHTY ALL-CAPS later claimed to be hacked, with the 'FBI Computer Crimes Department' currently on the lookout for the perpetrator.

The episode of Kitchen Nightmares ended with Gordon Ramsay, winner of 15 Michelin Stars, giving up on the two. While Samy spent most of the episode screaming at the patrons, stealing tips from the waitstaff and being a gangster like your average restaurant manager, Amy gave us valuable insight into the mind of a (possibly bipolar) "chef" and mother of babies in cats' bodies.

Sadly, not many of us will have the once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to speak with her personally. To simulate a conversation with Amy, we have created Chat with Amy. The chat-bot draws upon statements she made during the episode and the infamous Facebook meltdown to create a unique experience for you.
